December 4, 2024

Astronomers have discovered an extremely rare group of five 'dwarf' galaxies that are 'interacting with each other' about 117 million light years away from Earth Dwarf galaxies contain a few billion stars, whereas standard galaxies have hundreds of billions, giving them low mass and luminosity.The newly found galaxies are blue, gas-rich and actively forming new stars.
